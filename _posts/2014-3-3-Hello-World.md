My scholarly and applied interests converge at the frontier of blockchain economics, machine learning & neural networks, and digital asset valuation theory:

	•	Blockchain Network Valuation Framework: I formulate and empirically validate quantitative valuation models for decentralized networks, extending Metcalfe's Law through machine learning inference to elucidate the intrinsic value of digital assets, principally through building BNV Framework.

	•	Deep Learning in Financial Systems: I build GRU-Attention-CNN hybrid architectures to model digital asset price dynamics, synthesizing frontier representation learning with blockchain network theory to attain R² > 0.95 on Glassnode data (2015–2022).

**Story**

From the start of my career as an Investment Analyst and Investment Officer with PR Pension Plan, I had first-hand experience in building accurate financial models to track and monitor the performance of a $1.2B investment portfolio. After working in the finance realm for a few years and passing the CFA Level 1 exam, I decided to go a step further and pursue my Masters in Financial Technology at Strathclyde, which was the first university to launch a graduate program in FinTech (2017) and is ranked #6 in Accounting & Finance in the UK. And so, I followed my burning desire to uncover the holy grail, find a software program that automates business itself without intermediate of the human factor, and discover the paradigm shift blockchain technology in the UK. I then fell into the crypto rabbit hole during my masters and decided to do my thesis in blockchain Technology titled, ‘Viability of Blockchain in payments & the intrinsic value of an Ethereum Network’ with a London’s Company, which enables me to become a strategic blockchain analyst and worked with a blockchain company in Australia. 

Once El Salvador adopted Bitcoin as a legal tender causing a tectonic shift, I took a leap of faith and decided to conduct a blockchain Worldwide Tour called “100x Investments & beyond”. This Tour was inspired by the Vitalik Buterin story that before building Ethereum he traveled the world to meet Bitcoin developers and joint events of blockchain technology to be in the forefront of Blockchain Technology. I travelled to six top Summits & Hackathons across the globe gathering insights and research on promising early-stage projects and smart contact platforms that have the potential to enable 100x and beyond alpha within the investment paradigm, value investing. I met with and interviewed several industry leaders and OG developers and published in-depth investment insights reports on each summit I attended via medium. This tour was a life-changing experience that granted me the opportunity to uncover the next Ethereum & unparallel knowledge in blockchain technology, which I can now leverage in this role to discover untapped next 100x. 

Breathing constant creativity and evolution in Blockchain, co-authored a peer-reviewed research paper on digital asset valuation, published in the International Journal of Blockchain Technologies and Applications, in collaboration with Ken Alabi, Ph.D M.CAE, titled Refining Metcalfe’s Law for Digital Blockchain Network Valuation.

I may not think like Satoshi or deploy like Vitalik Buterin, but I can build and decipher valuation models for the intrinsic value of digital assets, like a hybrid of Warren Buffett and Jim Simons, so to speak. Most importantly, I keep Socrates' wisdom in mind: "I know that I know nothing," to stay grounded in this ever-evolving, fast-paced industry.
